Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026

A&A Trade Solutions ("we", "us", or "our") operates the A&A Catalog mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you place an order through the App. This includes:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Shipping address (street address, city, postcode, country)
  • The items you have ordered

We do not require you to create an account. All orders are placed as a guest. We do not collect payment information inside the App — payment is handled separately outside the App directly between you and the business.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Process and fulfil your order
  • Contact you regarding your order (shipping updates, payment arrangements)

We do not use your information for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. How Your Information Is Shared

Your order details are transmitted to our business team via email when you submit an order. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for their own purposes.

Order emails are sent using Gmail's SMTP service (Google LLC) as a delivery mechanism. Google may process message metadata in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

Order information is retained in our email inbox for as long as necessary to fulfil your order and comply with any applicable legal obligations. We do not store your data in a separate database.

5. Data Security

All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted in transit via HTTPS/TLS. We take reasonable measures to protect your information, though no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you by contacting us at the address below. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
